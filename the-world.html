The World

Acceptance is something the world is in need of currently; Even though there's a gaining population of our newer generation of people, there's still so much hatred people have on each other. This doesn't​ just mean race, but also ethnicity, religion, gender, and etc. There is strong hatred towards people simply because of their differences, how can one create a stronger community if they aren't accepted? Many horrible events of hate has happened, especially​ towards those of different genders. Many countries today don't allow the freedom as much as the United States provide, although it's something that can be changed. When people rise up, that is when change will happened. If we are to ever live in a fully free functional society, people are to unite and stand together not only for the freedom​ of the world but for it's unity.
